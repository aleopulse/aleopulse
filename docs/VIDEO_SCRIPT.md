# LeoPulse Video Demo Script

## Video Specifications

- **Duration**: 3-5 minutes
- **Format**: Screen recording with voiceover
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (1080p)

---

## Pre-Recording Checklist

- [ ] Clear browser history/cache
- [ ] Close unnecessary tabs
- [ ] Set browser zoom to 100%
- [ ] Prepare test wallets with credits and PULSE
- [ ] Verify Aleo testnet is operational
- [ ] Test microphone levels

---

## Script

### INTRO (0:00 - 0:20)

**[SCREEN: LeoPulse landing page]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "LeoPulse is a privacy-preserving polling platform built on Aleo where users can vote anonymously and earn real token rewards. Using zero-knowledge proofs, your vote choices stay private while remaining fully verifiable. Let's see how it works."

---

### WALLET CONNECTION (0:20 - 0:45)

**[SCREEN: Click Connect Wallet button]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Users connect with Aleo wallets like Leo Wallet. Unlike traditional polling platforms, LeoPulse ensures your privacy using Aleo's native ZK proof technology."

**[ACTION: Connect with Leo Wallet]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Once connected, you can participate in polls, create surveys, and earn rewards - all while maintaining privacy when you choose."

---

### PRIVACY MODES (0:45 - 1:15)

**[SCREEN: Show poll creation with privacy mode selector]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "LeoPulse offers three privacy modes. Poll creators choose the level that fits their use case."

**[ACTION: Highlight each mode]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Anonymous mode keeps both your identity and vote choice private using zero-knowledge proofs. Semi-private shows who voted but not what they chose. And Identified mode is fully transparent - perfect for governance votes where accountability matters."

---

### CREATING A POLL (1:15 - 2:00)

**[SCREEN: Navigate to Creator > Create Poll]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Let's create an anonymous poll."

**[ACTION: Fill in poll form]**
- Title: "What's the most important feature in a blockchain?"
- Options: "Privacy", "Speed", "Low Fees", "Security"
- Privacy Mode: Anonymous
- Duration: 1 hour
- Reward: 100 PULSE, Equal Split

**VOICEOVER:**
> "We set it to Anonymous mode - voters will prove their vote is valid without revealing their choice. The reward pool of 100 PULSE tokens will be split equally among all voters."

**[ACTION: Create poll and confirm transaction]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "The poll is now live on Aleo. The reward tokens are locked in the smart contract until distribution."

---

### VOTING ON A POLL (2:00 - 2:30)

**[SCREEN: Switch to participant view]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Now let's vote from a participant's perspective."

**[ACTION: Browse polls and vote]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "I can see this poll is in Anonymous mode - my vote choice will be private. When I vote, a zero-knowledge proof is generated that proves my vote is valid without revealing what I voted for."

**[ACTION: Confirm vote transaction]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "I receive a VoteReceipt - a private record that proves I participated. I can use this later to claim my reward."

---

### QUESTIONNAIRES (2:30 - 2:50)

**[SCREEN: Navigate to Questionnaires]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "LeoPulse also supports questionnaires - bundled surveys where participants complete multiple polls and share a reward pool."

**[ACTION: Show a questionnaire with multiple polls]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "All votes are submitted in a single transaction for efficiency, and rewards are distributed based on completion."

---

### STAKING & TIERS (2:50 - 3:10)

**[SCREEN: Navigate to Staking page]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Users stake PULSE tokens to unlock higher tiers. Higher tiers mean more daily votes."

**[ACTION: Show tier breakdown]**
- Bronze: 3 votes/day
- Platinum: 12 votes/day

**VOICEOVER:**
> "This creates a healthy token economy while rewarding engaged participants."

---

### GAS SPONSORSHIP (3:10 - 3:20)

**[SCREEN: Navigate to Settings]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "We're also building a gas sponsorship service using Aleo's native fee delegation - coming soon! This will enable free transactions for users."

---

### CLOSING (3:20 - 3:45)

**[SCREEN: Return to landing page or dashboard]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "LeoPulse brings together privacy-preserving polling, token rewards, and gamification - all powered by Aleo's zero-knowledge proof technology.

> Unlike other platforms, your vote choices can stay truly private while remaining cryptographically verifiable. Visit leopulse.xyz to try it yourself."

**[SCREEN: Show logo, website URL, social links]**

---

## Recording Tips

- Speak clearly and at moderate pace
- Pause briefly between sections
- Keep mouse movements smooth
- Avoid clicking too fast
- Emphasize "privacy" and "zero-knowledge proofs"
- Have backup recording ready
